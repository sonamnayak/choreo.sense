WHAT WE DO

At CHOREO.SENSE, our capabilities encompass a diverse range of artistic expressions that add a touch of enchantment and vibrancy to events.

01

COSTUME THEME CHOREOGRAPHY

Costume-themed choreography is a creative approach to dance routines where the dancers' costumes play a significant role in enhancing the overall performance. The chosen theme can inspire the movements, formations, and interactions within the choreography.The costumes are carefully chosen and designed to align with a specific theme or concept, adding depth, visual interest, and storytelling elements to the dance.

02

REGIONAL FOLK CHOREOGRAPHY

dance routines that are rooted in the traditional dances and cultural expressions of specific regions or countries. These dances have been passed down through generations and often reflect the unique customs, history, and identity of the region. Creating a regional folk choreography involves immersing oneself in the traditions and characteristics of the specific region. For example: Bhangra, Kalbeliya, Kashmiri, Bihu, Himachali, Garba etc.

03

INTERNATIONAL DANCE CHOREOGRAPHY

International dance choreography often aims to showcase the diversity and richness of global dance traditions while exploring innovative and contemporary approaches. Choreographers draw inspiration from a wide range of sources, including traditional folk dances, classical ballet, modern dance, hip-hop, Latin dances, African dances, Indian classical dance, and many others.

04

MUSICAL /THEATER PLAY

A musical theater play, also known as a musical, is a form of theatrical performance that combines spoken dialogue, acting, singing, and dancing with musical numbers and songs. Musicals are typically staged productions that tell a story through a combination of these elements, creating a cohesive and entertaining experience for the audience. Some examples of musicals are Mughal-e-Azam: The Musical, Krishna: The Musical, Mamma Mia!
